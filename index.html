Description: Project Allocation Systems are designed to manage and store project information that are used in web-based applications. By different groups of people such as, students, faculty or admin will be let by project applications a controlled access to information and automated distribution of information. The main objective of the student project allocation system is to make an internet based system which will provide information of each student to institute with ease.
